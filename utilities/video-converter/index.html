How it works

  • 100% Private: All video processing happens in your browser using WebAssembly. Nothing is uploaded to any server.
  • Powered by FFmpeg: Uses FFmpeg.wasm, a WebAssembly port of the popular FFmpeg multimedia framework.
  • First Load: The first time you convert, it will download ~30MB of libraries. Subsequent conversions are much faster.
  • Large Files: Very large videos (>500MB) may be slow or fail due to browser memory limitations.
