YouTube Downloader

Download YouTube videos directly in your browser. All processing happens client-side - no data is sent to any server.

Important Notice

  • Terms of Service: Downloading YouTube videos may violate YouTube's Terms of Service. This tool is for educational purposes and personal backups only.
  • Copyright: Respect content creators' rights. Only download videos you have permission to download.
  • Limitations: This tool may not work for all videos, especially age-restricted, private, or DRM-protected content.

How it works

  • Privacy-Focused: Uses the Invidious API (privacy-focused YouTube frontend) to fetch video data without tracking.
  • Client-Side Download: After fetching video info, downloads happen directly in your browser with no server uploads.
  • Supported Formats: Downloads available in MP4 and WebM formats with various quality options (360p, 720p, 1080p, etc.).
  • Audio Included: Most videos include both video and audio tracks. Some may only have video-only formats available.
  • Reliability: Uses multiple Invidious instances for better reliability. Much more stable than HTML parsing.
  • Limitations: May not work for age-restricted, private, or region-locked videos due to YouTube's restrictions.
